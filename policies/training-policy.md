# Training and Professional Development Policy

**Document ID:** HR-POL-006  
**Version:** 1.3  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Owner:** Human Resources Department  
**Last Reviewed:** November 28, 2023  

---

## 1. Purpose

Acme Corporation is committed to investing in the continuous growth and professional development of its employees. This policy outlines the training resources, reimbursement programs, and development opportunities available to employees, and sets expectations for how learning activities should be planned and tracked.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all regular full-time and part-time employees. Eligibility for specific programs may vary based on employment status, tenure, and role.

---

## 3. Learning and Development Budget

### 3.1 Individual Development Budget

Each eligible employee receives an annual **Learning and Development (L&D) budget of $1,500 per calendar year**. This budget may be used for:

- Online courses and learning platforms (e.g., Coursera, Udemy, LinkedIn Learning, Pluralsight)
- Professional certification exams and preparation materials
- Technical books and industry publications (up to $200/year within the budget)
- Conference registration fees (in conjunction with manager approval)
- Workshops and seminars directly related to the employee's role

The L&D budget resets on **January 1** each year and does not carry over. Unused budget cannot be converted to cash.

### 3.2 Budget for Managers and Above

Employees at the Manager level and above receive an enhanced L&D budget:

| Level               | Annual L&D Budget |
|---------------------|------------------|
| Manager             | $2,500           |
| Senior Manager      | $3,000           |
| Director            | $4,000           |
| VP and above        | $5,000           |

### 3.3 How to Access the Budget

1. Identify a relevant learning opportunity
2. Submit a Learning Request in Workday for pre-approval
3. Manager and HR review and approve the request within **5 business days**
4. Upon completion of the course/certification, submit receipt in Workday for reimbursement
5. Reimbursement is processed within **10 business days** of submission

For purchases under $100, employees may expense directly and submit within 30 days.

## 4. Company-Provided Training Resources

### 4.1 Internal Learning Management System (LMS)

Acme Corporation's LMS (accessible via lms.acmecorp.com) provides:

- **Mandatory compliance training** (security, ethics, harassment, data privacy)
- **Onboarding training modules** for new hires
- **Role-specific learning paths** developed by department heads and HR
- **Leadership development tracks** for employees aspiring to management roles
- **Soft skills courses** (communication, project management, presentation skills)

Employees may access the LMS at any time during work hours without using their personal L&D budget.

### 4.2 LinkedIn Learning

All Acme Corporation employees have free access to **LinkedIn Learning**, providing thousands of on-demand courses on technology, business, and creative skills. Access is available through the company's single sign-on (SSO) portal.

### 4.3 Internal Mentorship Program

Acme runs a formal **Mentorship Program** matching employees with senior leaders for 6-month mentorship cycles. Participants meet bi-monthly. Applications are open in **January and July** each year. Employees can apply as mentees after 6 months of employment. Senior employees are encouraged to apply as mentors.

---

## 5. Certification and Tuition Assistance

### 5.1 Professional Certification Support

For high-value professional certifications directly relevant to the employee's role, the company may cover costs exceeding the standard L&D budget with additional VP approval. Examples include:

- AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud certifications
- CPA, CFA, or legal bar examination fees
- PMP (Project Management Professional)
- Specialty engineering certifications

Employees who receive certification reimbursement above $2,000 must remain employed for at least **12 months** after receiving the benefit. Departure before 12 months requires repayment on a pro-rated basis.

### 5.2 Tuition Assistance

Acme Corporation offers tuition assistance for accredited degree programs (undergraduate or graduate) that are relevant to the employee's current or future role:

- **Maximum benefit:** $5,250 per calendar year (the IRS tax-exempt maximum)
- **Eligibility:** Employees must have completed at least **1 year of continuous employment** and be in good standing
- **Grade requirement:** Employees must achieve a grade of B or better (or equivalent passing grade)
- **Repayment clause:** Employees who leave within **24 months** of receiving tuition assistance must repay 50% of the benefit

Applications for tuition assistance must be submitted to HR and approved before the course begins. Retroactive approval is not available.

---

## 6. Conference Attendance

### 6.1 Approval

Attendance at external conferences (industry events, trade shows, technical summits) requires:

- Pre-approval from the employee's manager
- Alignment with the employee's development goals or business needs
- Registration fees covered from the L&D budget (or approved separately if exceeding the budget)
- Travel and accommodation costs covered by the Expense Policy (FIN-POL-001)

### 6.2 Knowledge Sharing

Employees who attend external conferences are required to:

- Share a brief summary (written or presentation) of key learnings with their team within **2 weeks** of returning
- Post a summary in the appropriate Slack channel (#learning-and-development)

## 7. Internal Training Programs

### 7.1 Management Development Program

The **Acme Manager Academy** is a 6-month internal program for employees transitioning into or new to management roles. It covers:

- Fundamentals of people management
- Performance conversations and feedback
- Hiring and interviewing best practices
- Employment law basics for managers

Enrollment is by nomination from a VP or HR. The program runs twice per year (starting in Q1 and Q3).

### 7.2 High-Potential Program (HiPo)

The **Acme HiPo Program** is a 12-month accelerated development program for employees identified as high performers with leadership potential. HiPo participants receive:

- Executive mentorship
- Cross-functional project assignments
- Priority access to leadership development resources
- Participation in strategic planning discussions

Nominations are made during the annual performance review cycle. Selection is by the Senior Leadership Team.

---

## 8. Time for Learning

### 8.1 Dedicated Learning Time

Employees are encouraged to dedicate at least **4 hours per month** to learning and development activities during working hours, with manager awareness. This is not a formal entitlement but a cultural expectation supported by leadership.

### 8.2 Study Leave

Employees taking professional exams (e.g., certifications, bar exam, CPA) may request up to **3 days of paid study leave** per exam, in addition to PTO. Study leave must be approved by the manager and submitted in Workday at least 4 weeks in advance.

---

## 9. Development Planning

### 9.1 Individual Development Plan (IDP)

All employees are encouraged to maintain an **Individual Development Plan (IDP)** in Workday, updated at least annually. The IDP outlines:

- Career goals (1-year and 3-year horizon)
- Skills to develop
- Planned learning activities and resources
- Milestones and timelines

IDPs are discussed during the annual performance review and mid-year check-in.
